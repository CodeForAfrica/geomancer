What is Geomancer?

Geomancer is an open-source tool for journalists to easily add context to existing data, by finding & combining other relevant datasets by location.

To get started, all you need is a dataset that includes a column with geographic information, like city or state.

Geomancer is funded by a Prototype Grant from the Knight Foundation.
